Ti’buro was created for the French competition «La Camif 2010». This modular desk is for elementary schools and allows students to do different activities like working, painting... One of the faces of the work surface is a blackboard to do some addition in math for example. When you order the Ti’buro furniture, you can take 2 or 4 desks more to create storage. In fact, the Ti’buro can become storage thanks to the different parts of the chair that you can put on the desk to create shelves.

Ti’buro is made in Durat plastic. This plastic is an ecological material which contains recycled postindustrial plastics and can be 100% recyclable. This plastic is often used for bathtub and kitchen, so it is waterproof and resistant for pencil mark or the scuffs. Project realized with Clarisse Simonneau.
